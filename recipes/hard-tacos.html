Ingredients

  • Corn Tortillas
  • Shredded Chicken
  • Shredded Cheese
  • Vegatable Oil
  • Shredded Lettace
  • Choice of Toppings

Steps

  1. Warm up corn tortillas. 1 at a time for over-the-stove and 4 at a time for 60 seconds in the microwave.
  2. With desired amount of warm tortillas on the side, prep your tacos. First putting aa pinch of shredded chese in the middle of the tortilla, then a pinch of shredded chicken on top of the cheese.
  3. Fold the taco and put it on the side.
  4. Repeat step two and three for desired amount of tacos.
  5. Warm up vegatable oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  6. Place prepped tacos in the warm oil, one at a time.
  7. Fry until tortillas brown than flip.
  8. Once both sides are browned, remove from pan and let cool over paper towel.
  9. Place lettace and desired toppings inside the tacos.
  10. Enjoy your Tacos!
