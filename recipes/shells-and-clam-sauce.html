Ingredients

  • 1 cup shell noodle pasta
  • 4 cup water
  • 1 can chopped clams
  • 1 can Progresso clam sauce
  • 1 can crab meat
  • 1 can canned shrimp

Steps

  1. Bring 4 cups water to a rapid boil in an uncovered pot.
  2. Stir in 1 cup shells into boiling water for 10 minutes or until shells are tender. Stir occasionally.
  3. In a separate pot, combine the can of chopped clams, clam sauce, crab meat, and shrimp. Heat over medium heat.
  4. Once pasta is done, strain over sink and place into a bowl.
  5. Combine sauce and pasta
  6. Enjoy!
