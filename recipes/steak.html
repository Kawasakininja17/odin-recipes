Ingredients

  • 1 London Broil Steak
  • 2 tbsp White Wine Vinegar
  • Season Salt
  • Steak Seasoning
  • 4 Russel Potatoes
  • 2 cup Milk
  • 1 Stick Butter
  • Asparagus

Steps

  1. Turn oven to bake.
  2. Place raw london broil steak on top of a baking sheet with aluminum foil.
  3. pour 2 tbsp of white wine vinegar over the steak, and sprinkle season salt, and steak seasoning over the steak.
  4. Tenderize seasoned steak with a fork and place in the oven for 20 minutes, flipping halfway through.
  5. While steak bakes, skin and chop for russel potatoes.
  6. Bring chopped potatoes to a boil in a large pot.
  7. Chop the whites off of the end of the asparagus.
  8. Saut√© asparagus in 1/4th cup butter over medium heat.
  9. Once potatoes are soft and tender, strain water from pot.
  10. Mash potatoes, 2 cup milk, and remaining butter, until mashed potatoes reach desired consistency.
  11. Once timer is done, take steak out of the oven and let sit for five minutes.
  12. Serve plate and enjoy!
